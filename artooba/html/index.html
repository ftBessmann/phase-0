Community of Art Professionals

Artooba connects art professionals with public places worldwide to showcase art for sale. The display is an excellent opportunity to increase your local exposure and be noticed by collectors and curators. The platform provides you with all sorts of optional services including shipping, insurance, installation, aderting and payment processing. But most important, you are valued as a member of an open and equitable ecosystem that grows with you.

Become Our Member

Global Network of Public Places

Hospitality businesses, financial institutions, shopping malls and other alternate venues represent an essential part of the Artooba ecosystem. By hosting exhibitions public places gain two huge advantages. First, the art doesn’t end up costing you anything. Second, you are not stuck with the art like you would be if you had purchased it. Every few months you can change the exhibition and have a totally new art collection to share with your customers.
