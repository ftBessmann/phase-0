Artooba connects artists with public places (hotels, bars, restaurants, night clubs, shopping malls, spas and beauty salons, hospitals, city halls, libraries, banks, offices) to showcase their artworks for sale. Artists lack non-discriminatory access to art buyers. Art intermediaries maintain artificial entry barriers to the market. In the opinion of the majority of art lovers, art dealers keep the prices for contemporary art unreasonably high. The Artooba platform disrupts the traditional gallery model and creates an open and equitable marketplace of a new kind. By doing so, we increase societal capacities to express ideas and to innovate for economic, civic, and individual benefits.
